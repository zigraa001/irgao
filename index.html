The Challenge

Why India Needs a
New Mobility Layer

Every day, millions of hours are lost to congestion, delayed access, and fragmented transport systems. For urgent travel, premium mobility, and time-critical movement, today's ground transport networks are often too slow, too uncertain, and too constrained.

Congestion

Millions of hours lost in traffic daily, costing India ₹2.1 lakh crore annually in economic productivity.

Delays

Unpredictable travel times impact lives and businesses. Emergency response averages 14+ minutes in Tier-2 cities.

Limited Access

Remote and high-value routes remain underserved. India needs a faster, more reliable mobility alternative.

The Solution

Introducing IraGo

An AI-powered air mobility platform designed to connect people and places through eVTOL-enabled journeys.

IraGo is developing the digital and operational backbone for next-generation aerial mobility in India. Our platform is built to support route optimization, scheduling intelligence, and fleet visibility in real time, enabling efficient operations across emergency response, urgent travel, and high-value passenger movement.
